Appetizer Recipes

1. Garlic Bread

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Mix butter and garlic, spread on bread, bake for 10 minutes, and garnish with parsley.

2. Spring Rolls

  • Rice paper
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce

Fill rice paper with veggies, roll tightly, and serve with soy sauce.
